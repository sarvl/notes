## Safety
Differently to [[Multimeter]], [[Ground]] in oscilloscope is actually connected to Mains ground. This means that the ground of the probe has to *always* connect to Mains ground too, otherwise short circuit is created. Consequently, different BNC connectors are also connected between oscilloscopes, so only one ground can be used at once, even in circuits without access to mains, though in such a case ground can be connected wherever.
One solution to this is usage of Differential Probes.
