## Capacity
Capacity is how much current can be supplied for given time. Proper unit would be [[Energy|Joule]], not [[Charge|Amp Hour]]. However capacity is usually denoted in amp hour.


All batteries have some discharge curve that dictates how [[Voltage]] degrades over time with constant current.

Batteries store a lot of energy, but they are limited by series resistance.

## Charging
![[Battery_Charging.png]]

There are 3 modes
- precharge
  Used to ensure the voltage is high enough to start constant current, rejuvenating battery from really  dead state
- constant current
- constant voltage
