#networking 

Ethernet is a protocol operating in [[Computer_Network#TCP/IP|Link]] layer, it creates broadcast network where all packets are sent everywhere (unless a [[Switch]] is introduced). 
A separation of [[Computer_Network|Networks]] is very useful for [[Cybersecurity]] but it is also very costly and not flexible, for this reason [[VLAN|VLANs]] are introduced.


## Devices
- Network Interface Card
- Repeater
- Hub
- Bridge
- [[Switch]]
