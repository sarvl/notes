#science 


Fundamentally fossil fuels require constant search for new resources which are then only used once and are gone forever. For this reason grid based on renewable energy is more sustainable.  Furthermore, as Sun shines and Wind blows everywhere, they are not as dependent on current geopolitical situation. Even rare metal [[Battery|Batteries]] can be recycled yielding almost all the materials back, after all, dead battery is the richest source of materials to make another battery.

Most of our needs are already met by solution that does not depend on fossil fuels directly (Electric Grid), therefore all we have to do is to switch power source. 

Major advantage of electric vehicles is that  they can be charged overnight.

Right now, renewable power is insanely cheaper than fossil fuel power. Especially for solar panels, which are a solid state device, and require almost no maintenance to keep functioning, once their cost has been paid off (which also is low) generate almost pure profit.

Regarding land use, its usage would actually *decrease* if we were to switch to all renewables, because *more* land is used for crops used purely for ethanol used to improve fuel. Even more, the land can actually be used for other purposes.  Obviously with wind farms which can be installed among crop fields, but Agrivoltaics might even increase the yield of some crops.

Most of the panel is composed out of glass and aluminum, all are already easily recyclable, so almost the entire panel is recyclable.

[[Nuclear_Power|Nuclear Power]], while great, has disadvantage of taking very long to deploy, which slows down transition away from Fossil Fuels for [[Climate_Change|Climate Change]].
