#networking #computer_architecture 

A particular instance of a [[Network]] on a [[System_On_A_Chip|SoC]]. 

It has an advantage of very low latency connections and no pin constraints. But due to structure of substrate, it essentially forces 2D topologies. 
The main concern is energy and power consumption.
