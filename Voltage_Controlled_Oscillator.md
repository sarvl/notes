#analog #electronics 

VCO is a type of [[Multivibrators|Oscillator]] that has frequency controlled by voltage.

Most VCOs have varactor [[Diode|Diodes]].

Such oscillators should be monotonic.

Frequency Pushing - how output frequency changes as the supply voltage changes.
Frequency Pulling - how output frequency changes as the load changes.
