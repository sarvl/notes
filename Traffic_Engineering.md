#networking 

Measurement and prediction of amount of traffic is crucial to proper design of a [[Network]].  Such process can be carried at any level of abstraction provided by particular Network.

Traffic is flow of any communication primitive. 
Traffic load is ratio of the device occupancy time to the observation time.
Traffic intensity is the ratio of busy times to the duration of that period.

A unit of traffic is Erlang, defined to be occupancy of 1 unit of time during observation time of 1 unit of time.
