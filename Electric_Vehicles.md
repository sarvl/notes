#science 

EVs have massive advantage over petrol cars - they can be charged at home during night with electricity prices, this makes them *much* cheaper and more convenient than petrol cars.

While there are special chargers, they are not necessary for day-to-day operation. The DC fast charges generally are only needed for trips.

The range drops in winter, mainly due to heating, but as long as the daily commute is reasonable, it's not very noticeable. This can be further improved with [[Heat_Pump|Heat Pumps]].
